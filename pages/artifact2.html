Potential Options for best artifact

For Showcase 2, I will feature my involvement in the Design for Dignity project, which began as a class project focused on improving health and wellness resources for elderly individuals in collaboration with the Boner Community Center. What started as an academic assignment evolved into a larger, community-centered initiative. Through this experience, our work moved beyond the classroom and became part of a broader effort to support elderly populations through thoughtful, research-driven design. I later contributed to developing the Design for Dignity website, helping transform the project into a more permanent and professional digital platform. This experience stands out to me because it demonstrates how I can take a class-based concept and expand it into something with real-world impact.

Artifact(s) I Plan to Include To document and illustrate my learning, I plan to include:

  • Visuals from the original class project (brochures, posters, or research materials related to elderly health)
  • Screenshots of the Design for Dignity website
  • Development process work (wireframes, design mockups, or code screenshots)
  • Any documentation showing the evolution from classroom project to community-focused initiative.

    • These artifacts will demonstrate both the original research and design work as well as the technical development that helped bring the project to a broader audience.
