Retrospective Reflection

Throughout my time in the IU Indianapolis Honors College, I have grown into a more thoughtful, creative, and community-minded designer. My Honors experiences including international collaboration, applied web development, and leadership have shaped how I approach design, storytelling, and problem-solving.

"My Yonsei University collaboration is evidence that my work achieves the IUI Honors College goal of participating in engaged learning experiences."

Honors Goal: Active Learning

Be active participants in intellectual experiences.

Through projects like Design for Dignity and my web development coursework, I moved beyond completing assignments to intentionally designing experiences that communicate meaning and impact.

Honors Goal: Engaged Learning

Participate in engaged learning experiences.

My Yonsei University collaboration and Web Developer Internship allowed me to apply my skills in real-world and cross-cultural contexts, strengthening both my technical and collaborative abilities.

Yonsei University Collaboration

This cross-cultural project focused on food insecurity and required collaboration with international students. I later expanded this work into a website used to showcase student work, strengthening my skills in design, storytelling, and global communication.

Design for Dignity Website

Working with a senior designer and industry professional, I contributed to both frontend and backend development. This experience gave me insight into professional workflows and reinforced the importance of collaboration in design.

Web Developer Internship

My internship with IU Indianapolis allowed me to apply HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in a real-world setting, strengthening my problem-solving skills and understanding of user-centered design.

Growth as a Designer and Scholar

Throughout my Honors experience, I developed stronger communication, leadership, and technical skills. As a Teaching Assistant and OTEAM co-leader, I learned how to guide others, explain complex ideas, and create supportive learning environments.

My Teaching Assistant role is evidence that my work achieves the IUI Honors College goal of developing strong communication and problem-solving skills. It required me to adapt my explanations and support students with different learning styles.

These experiences helped me shift from simply completing assignments to creating purposeful, user-centered designs that reflect both technical skill and meaningful intent.

"My Teaching Assistant experience is evidence that my work achieves the IUI Honors College goal of developing communication and problem-solving skills."

Looking Ahead

As I prepare to graduate, I see myself as an emerging web and graphic designer with a strong foundation in collaboration, storytelling, and user experience. The Honors College has helped shape both my skills and my perspective, preparing me for a meaningful career in design.
